SUPPORT & FAQ

Find answers to common questions and get support.

How do I connect to the server?

Use Minecraft versions 1.8.x through 1.20.x and connect to play.degesmc.qzz.io. Make sure to register your account with /register [password] for security.

How do I purchase ranks?

Visit our store section or use /buy in-game. All purchases support server development and provide exclusive perks.

I lost my items! What do I do?

Contact staff immediately in Discord or use /helpop. Keep screenshots of your inventory before the incident for faster resolution.

How do I report a player?

Use /report [player] [reason] in-game or contact staff in our Discord server. Provide as much detail as possible.

Can I transfer my rank to another account?

Rank transfers are not available. Each account must purchase ranks separately. Contact support if you have special circumstances.
